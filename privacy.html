Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how vimark.art collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website and Pinterest integration features.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data directly from visitors. The site uses standard analytics tools (Google Analytics and Yandex.Metrika) to understand traffic patterns. These services may use cookies and collect anonymized IP addresses.

2. Pinterest Integration

vimark.art uses the Pinterest API to automatically share portfolio artwork to Pinterest boards. When you visit a project page, Pinterest Rich Pins metadata is provided to enable proper attribution and linking. We do not store Pinterest user data on our servers.

3. Cookies

We use minimal cookies for theme preferences (light/dark mode) and analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

4. Third-Party Services

Our site may contain links to third-party websites (Behance, DeviantArt, LinkedIn, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information transmitted through the site. Pinterest API tokens and credentials are stored securely via GitHub Secrets and are never exposed in public repositories.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@vimark.art.
