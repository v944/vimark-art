Vegetation

2026 · Personal

A series of illustrations for Ivanov’s Vegetation. The project visualizes a world where the boundary between the human and the botanical has not just blurred — it has been overgrown. The images follow the logic of rampant ecology: architecture devoured by ivy and moss, figures half-emerged from bark and pulp, and landscapes where photosynthesis has replaced thought.
