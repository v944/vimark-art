About

I'm Max Mitenkov, an award-winning illustrator and concept artist with 12+ years in the entertainment industry. My work spans book covers, AAA game environments, and NFT character design — created with Photoshop, ZBrush, and Unreal Engine 5. I've collaborated with studios in Dubai (Apex Digital), Los Angeles (ICVR), and Minsk (Lunas Pro). Currently open to freelance and full-time opportunities.
